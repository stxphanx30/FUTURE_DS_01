# 📊 Business Sales Performance Analysis

## 🧭 Introduction

This analysis explores the sales performance of an online retail business using transactional data.  
The objective is to identify key revenue drivers, understand sales trends, and provide actionable recommendations to support business growth.

The analysis includes data cleaning, anomaly handling, and visualization through a Power BI dashboard.

---

## 🔍 Data Preparation & Methodology

Before conducting the analysis, the dataset was carefully cleaned to ensure accuracy:

- Missing Customer IDs were retained and labeled as "Unknown" to preserve all transactions
- Cancelled transactions and negative values were removed
- Non-product entries such as **POSTAGE, MANUAL adjustments, DISCOUNT, and AMAZON fees** were identified and excluded
- Outliers were analyzed using statistical methods and business logic to avoid removing valid bulk purchases

This approach ensures that insights are based on **real business activity** rather than misleading data.

---

## 📊 Key Findings

### 💰 Revenue Performance

The business generated approximately **£10.18 million in total revenue**, reflecting strong overall performance and consistent transaction activity.

---

### 📈 Sales Trend

Revenue demonstrates a **clear upward trend throughout the year**, with a significant peak in **October and November**.

This indicates strong **seasonal demand**, likely driven by holiday shopping behavior.

---

### 🌍 Geographic Performance

The **United Kingdom dominates revenue generation**, contributing the majority of total sales.

This highlights a **high dependency on a single market**, with limited international revenue contribution.

---

### 🏆 Product Performance

A small number of products account for a large portion of total revenue, following the **Pareto principle (80/20 rule)**.

This suggests that business performance is heavily influenced by a **core set of high-performing products**.

---

### 👤 Customer Behavior

The business serves over **4,300 customers** and processes approximately **20,000 orders**, indicating a **high transaction volume model**.

This suggests frequent purchases and potentially strong customer engagement.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 🚀 1. Strengthen Top Product Strategy

Focus on high-performing products by increasing inventory availability, optimizing pricing, and investing in targeted marketing campaigns.

---

### 🌍 2. Expand International Presence

Reduce reliance on the UK market by developing strategies to grow sales in underperforming regions through localization and targeted promotions.

---

### 📅 3. Leverage Seasonal Opportunities

Prepare for peak demand periods (especially **Q4**) by aligning inventory, marketing campaigns, and logistics to maximize revenue.

---

### 🎯 4. Improve Product Diversification

Encourage sales across a broader product range to reduce dependency on a limited number of top-performing items.

---

### 👥 5. Enhance Customer Retention

Implement loyalty programs, personalized offers, and customer engagement strategies to increase repeat purchases and lifetime value.

---

## ✅ Conclusion

This analysis highlights key revenue drivers, customer behavior patterns, and growth opportunities.

By leveraging data-driven insights, the business can:

- Optimize product strategy
- Expand into new markets
- Improve customer retention
- Maximize seasonal performance

The accompanying Power BI dashboard provides an interactive view of these insights, enabling informed decision-making at both operational and strategic levels.
